Operation Paintball

The Relationship between bullet velocity and penetration:

During my time at the Southend High School for Boys, I was tasked with completing an artefact for my Computer Science A Level. It was then that I decided to migrate more towards Games Programming. I elected to create a first-person shooter from scratch, and without help from teachers, on the Unity Engine using a brand new language at the time. I was successful in developing an early stage simulation in which the player can use a multitude of weapons to shoot enemies that seek and chase the player, using a variety of common gameplay mechanics such as stamina and an ammo count.
I am proud to say that this project recieved the maximum possible marks due to its complexity and high standard, especially when looking at the excellent documentation that covers every aspect of development and how my learning was informed.
